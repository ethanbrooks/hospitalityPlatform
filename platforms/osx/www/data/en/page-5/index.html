Beautiful Garbage Tanks Flood Casco Viejo, Panama

Panama, Casco Viejo, Casco Antiguo Yesterday, under the rain, a group of residents in Casco Antiguo went out &#8220;guerrilla style&#8221; and distributed 18 beautiful garbage tanks decorated by Panamanian artists.  Like &#8220;positive art bombs&#8221;, the guerrilla group moved swiftly through A Avenue, in an almost military operation to inject the historic city with fun urban creativity. This project belongs to AVACA, Casco Antiguo`s Neighborhood and Friend`s Association, an organization that aims to see Casco Viejo become one of the greatest historic districts of the World.  From beach cleanings, to planting palms, Neighborhood Watch Program and even serve as a watchman for law enforcement, AVACA is usually involved in most of the community activities in Casco Antiguo. The goal with this &#8220;Art -Tank&#8221; project is to help educate the public into disposing the garbage correctly, while providing with much needed tanks and beautifying the streets by injecting some great urban art.  Yesterday, already under the rain, locals were pointing out the cans and saying &#8220;look! look! wao!&#8221;. The cans were so pretty they thought twice about throwing garbage inside! AVACA aims to collect enough funding to create 200 cans and be able to cover neighborhoods like Santa Ana.  So far,  in less than two months they`ve reached 40 cans and enthusiasm has grown. Thumbs up to all the people who have helped and continue to help in this beautiful project!

Beautiful World Pride Celebration

Panama, Casco Viejo, Casco Antiguo &nbsp; On Sunday was the final day of a full month of activities organized by World Pride Panama, an organization that raises awarenes for the LGBT community in our country. The activities included workshops open to everyone, from personal branding to corporate and legal. They even had a job fair that was a full success. The big event, though, was the march on Sunday.  It ended with a fun concert at Plaza V Centenario. Casco´s streets were dressed in rainbows, and happy energy flooded the airwaves.  It was a beautiful moment for everyone who came to participate and support.
