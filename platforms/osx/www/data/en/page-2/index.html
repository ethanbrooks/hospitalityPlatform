Amazing Community Week

Panama Casco Viejo, Casco Antiguo Last Wednesday, young families in Casco gathered at 5th street to inaugurate Casco´s first &#8220;baby park&#8221;, fully created and implemented by the community. The ngo that made it happen was AVACA, Casco´s neighborhood association.  The event was honored by the presence of the Major and the Director of Historic Patrimony, who have been known for working for a greener and friendlier city.  The park is free and fully open to the public. They also celebrated the third year of Day at the Bay, an initiative to bring awareness to Casco´s beach as a natural resource for the city and a family space for the community. Congratulations to everyone who participated! to learn more about  AVACA and their community work:   Facebook   Website: www.avaca.org.pa

Amazing Success! A Day at the Bay

Panama Casco Viejo, Casco Antiguo Last Saturday, neighbors and members of Casco Viejo`s neighborhood association (AVACA) gathered at the Santo Domingo beach to have fun. Do beach-like things. Simply use it. And the result was nothing short of a historic event.  Remember my post about this beach being &#8220;alive&#8221; back in the 40`s? (here) this was way better! American Trade Hotel in Casco Viejo Opens its Doors!

Panama, Casco Viejo, Casco Antiguo Simply the most ambitious and beautiful restoration done to date in Casco Viejo.  A delicate blend of Panama`s tropical culture with contemporary style.  Inside and outside connect through triple height glass windows, lush tropical plants inside echo with Plaza Herrera`s big trees.  Thoughtful details everywhere, my favorite being the old style library, where books have been cherry picked by the owners evoking the golden era of travel, back when it really meant exploring. You`ll find books from pioneers telling their tales of joy, struggle and wonder. You`ll also find a pool, a gym, and a jazz bar (opening soon!). I should do a post only on the American Trade History. Amici Miei , your late night option!

Panama, Casco Viejo, Casco Antiguo Last Friday we went out with some friends to the newly opened (beautiful and with great stuff!) Stranger´s Club. From there we went to the also newly opened El Social, a beer garden just next door. By the time we were finished there we were hungry again. Anti Corruption Film Festival in Casco Viejo

Panama Casco Viejo, Casco Antiguo Nothing like art and culture to open a dialogue and change society.  The current initiative by Panama´s Chapter of International Transparency paralel to the current International Anti Corruption Conference being held is simply genius (and of course we love it is happening in Casco´s plazas, so full of symbolism and open for free to everyone). Started November 29th and will continue until the 4th of December, mainly showing at Plaza Catedral, but the 3rd at Plaza Francia. 6:30 p.m.  Each night, a different movie, from Panama, Mexico and so many others, all with the same running theme, different angles. Each one an excellent piece.  I caught the one about the Dietilene Glycol case and it was painful, but also a story of courage and love.
